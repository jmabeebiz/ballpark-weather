Roadmap

What's coming next.

Ballpark Weather is a work in progress. Here's what I'm thinking about building — roughly in order of priority. If something here matters to you, or there's something missing that should be on the list, let me know on the feedback page.

🔍
Detailed game view
A dedicated page for each game with the full weather breakdown — hourly forecasts, wind direction, humidity, visibility, and more. Everything you'd want to know before driving to the park.
🏟️
Team and ballpark pages
Browse by team or stadium. See the full schedule for any team with weather risk baked in, so you can plan ahead for a series or a whole road trip.
📊
Historical delay and postponement data
Some ballparks get rained out far more than others. Historical patterns — by stadium, by month, by weather type — could make the risk scores sharper and give you better context for what you're looking at.
🎨
Team logos
A small thing that would make the matchup cards a lot more readable at a glance. Team abbreviations work, but logos are faster.
⚙️
Better scoring model
The current model is a solid starting point, but there's room to improve. Incorporating historical postponement data, stadium-specific quirks, and how different weather combinations interact would get the risk scores closer to how teams actually make these calls.
